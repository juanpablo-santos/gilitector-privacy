3. Data We Collect

3.1. Location Data

The Application may request access to your device's geolocation services (GPS). Location data is used exclusively to:

  • Calculate proximity to user-configured "A-HOLE-AREAS".
  • Display a geolocated map as a visual background during the detection animation.
  • Adapt humorous texts based on proximity.
Important: Location data is processed exclusively on the user's device. It is not sent, stored or shared with any external server of our own. The Application works correctly without location access; in that case, proximity-based features are automatically disabled.

3.3. Data Collected by Third Parties

The Application displays interstitial ads through Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect certain device data in accordance with its own privacy policy, including:

  • Device advertising identifiers (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android).
  • IP address.
  • Device information (model, operating system, version).
  • Ad interaction data.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy and How Google uses data from apps.

Users can permanently remove ads via an in-app purchase. In that case, the Google AdMob SDK becomes inactive and no data is shared with advertising networks.

3.4. In-App Purchases

The Application offers a non-consumable purchase to permanently remove ads. Purchase transactions are processed entirely through Google Play (Android) or App Store (iOS). The Application does not collect, store or have access to financial data, payment methods or transaction information. Only a boolean flag indicating whether the purchase was made is stored locally on the device.

For more information about how payments are processed, see the privacy policies of Google or Apple.

3.5. Map Data

The Application uses map tiles from OpenStreetMap to display locations. Tile requests are made directly to OpenStreetMap servers, which may log the user's IP address in accordance with their own privacy policy. No additional personally identifiable information is sent in these requests.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

The Application does not collect or access:

  • Name, email address or user contact information.
  • Message content, photos, files or communications.
  • Browsing history or activity in other applications.
  • Biometric data (fingerprint, facial recognition).
  • Financial information or payment methods.
  • Health or fitness data.

10. Security
